INFORMATION ABOUT EXO



EXO is a Kpop boyband group under SM Entertainment agency currently consisting of 9 members which are Sehun, Suho, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, Xiumin, D.O., Lay, and Kai (from left to right in the picture above). Their group name is derived from “Exoplanet”. EXO originally have 12 members separated between two groups which are EXO-K and EXO-M and debuted in 2012. But in 2014, the former members of EXO M such as Kris Wu, Luhan, and TAO left EXO and SM Entertainment, leaving EXO as a nine-member group. They have been active as a single group since 2014 while continuing to release and perform music in multiple languages.

Some of the EXO members formed subunits. In 2016, three members, Chen, Baekhyun and Xiumin, formed a subunit named EXO-CBX, incorporating the initials of each member's stage names. Chanyeol and Kai also created a subunit called EXO-SC. As of now (November 2021), 4 members of EXO (Chen, Suho, Chanyeol, and Baekhyun) are in the military. Despite of this, I hope that you'll continue to support EXO!
