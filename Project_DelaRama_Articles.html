Coming Soon: EXO's Kai's Comeback



SM Entertainment just confirmed two weeks ago that EXO's Kai is currently preparing for his solo comeback on November this year. On November 11, SM further confirmed that Peaches, Kai's second mini-album will be out on November 30.

This is the second time Kai will be having a comeback. He just released his solo mini-album last year, featuring his hit lead single 'Mmmh', so his fans are curious on what he will do next.


This news truly brought joy and excitement to his fans. Let's all support Kai in his upcoming comeback and activities!
